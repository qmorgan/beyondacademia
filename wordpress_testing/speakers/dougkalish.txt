Doug Kalish
Consultant, entrepreneur, and author of www.dougsguides.com
dougkalish.jpg
Doug Kalish is a Visiting Scholar at UC-Berkeley, with a PhD in biology from Harvard and more than 30 years of management experience. Kalish has advised the information technology and biotechnology industries on a range of issues, from knowledge and content management to collaboration and conflict resolution. He now devotes most of his time touring college campuses helping students make the transition from academia to the business world, and shares his experience and knowledge at http://www.dougsguides.com.
