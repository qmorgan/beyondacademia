An-Chi Tsou
Fellow at California Council on Science and Technology
an-chitsou.jpg
An-Chi Tsou received her Ph.D. in Bioengineering from the UC Berkeley-UCSF Bioengineering Graduate Program and her B.S. in Engineering Science from Smith College. Her research focused on designing optimal microenvironments for differentiation of stem cells into blood. She is currently a 2012-2013 CCST Fellow, where she works at the office of assembly member Bonta.
