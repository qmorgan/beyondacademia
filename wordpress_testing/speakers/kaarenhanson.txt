Kaaren Hanson
Vice President of Design Innovation at Intuit
kaarenhanson.jpg
Kaaren Hanson is the Vice President of Design Innovation at Intuit. Kaaren has deep expertise in Innovation and Design leadership. Her team balances inspiration and rigor to create experiences and inspirational prototypes that delight customers and lead to new behaviors across the company.  Kaaren’s success in changing Intuit’s innovation culture was highlighted in the June 2011 issue of HBR.  Kaaren also received Intuit's 2010 and 2011 CEO Leadership Awards for her efforts.  She earned her PhD in social psychology at Stanford University.
