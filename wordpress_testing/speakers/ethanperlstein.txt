Ethan Perlstein
Independent scientist and founder of Perlstein Lab
ethanperlstein.jpg
Ethan O. Perlstein, PhD., is an independent scientist and founder of Perlstein Lab, B-corp, a mission-driven biotech startup in the San Francisco Bay Area that focuses on personalized drug discovery for rare/orphan diseases. From 2007-2012, Ethan was an independent postdoctoral fellow at Princeton University, where his lab developed a novel approach to drug discovery called evolutionary pharmacology. Ethan received a PhD in molecular and cellular biology from Harvard University in 2006, and a BA in sociology from Columbia College in 2001. Ethan is originally from South Florida, and now resides in Oakland CA.
