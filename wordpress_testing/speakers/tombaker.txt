Tom Baker
Project leader at BCG
tombaker.jpg
Thomas Baker is a project leader at the San Francisco office of the Boston Consulting Group (BCG). At BCG, his focus and expertise is energy, with a specific focus on clean-tech, working with clients on topics ranging from solar to energy efficiency. Prior to joining BCG, Thomas worked in the strategic marketing department of First Solar and prior to that as a post-doctoral researcher in the Martin Head-Gordon group in the chemistry department at UC Berkeley. Thomas holds a Ph.D. in chemical physics from Harvard University and a B.S. in chemistry from Indiana University of Pennsylvania.
