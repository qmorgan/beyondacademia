Eva Amsen
Outreach director at F1000Research
evaamsen.jpg
Eva Amsen is the Outreach Director for F1000Research, an open science journal offering rapid publication, transparent peer review (post-publication) and full data deposition and sharing. Eva holds a PhD in Biochemistry from the University of Toronto, and is interested in all aspects of communication between researchers, from hallway conversations to academic papers. Before joining F1000Research, she launched and ran several initiatives for scientists to connect with each other and the wider community, both for academic publishers and in independent collaborations.
