Zoë Harris is a Senior Product Manager on the Avastin Marketing Team at Genentech focused on strategy and promotions for lung and colorectal cancers. Zoë began at Genentech in 2010 and completed the Commercial Rotation Development Program (CRDP), where she was part of the Government Affairs, Managed Care Marketing, and Government Price Reporting groups. Her experiences at Genentech range from implementing provisions of the Affordable Care Act, to launching a new oncology drug for metastatic melanoma.

Prior to Genentech, Zoë completed her PhD in Health Services & Policy Analysis (HSPA) at UC Berkeley, received her MPH from Yale, and a BA from University of Pennsylvania in Economics.
