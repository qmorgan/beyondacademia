Jesse Noffsinger
Engagement Manager at McKinsey
jessenoffsinger.gif
Jesse holds a Ph.D. in Physics from the University of California, Berkeley where his primary research focus was first-principles calculations of the electron-phonon interaction in metals and semiconductors.  He has been with McKinsey & Company for three years, and is currently an Engagement Manager at their Seattle office.  At McKinsey he has focused his work on improving operations at manufacturing and transport companies, although he has spent time in a broad array of other industries including healthcare, high tech, energy, financial, and basic materials.
